PRIMARY

The Tuition fees for june and the first term fees must be paid before the end of May. This applies even to those who are seeking financial assistance from the school. Those in receipt of Government Concession are promoted to the next higher class need not pay fees.

Fees will be paid through the South Indian Bank. Fee bills will be available online which parents can print form the website and pay to bank by cash.

Where possible, parents are requested to pay fees in advance for six months of year. If the fees for the month are not paid before 10th of the month, the boy is liable to be sent home. If fees cannot be paid in time Principle's or Vice Principal's permission must be obtained in writting in your application when you will be able to pay the fees. If the fees, are not paid wihtin 2 months the name will be struck off rolls.
